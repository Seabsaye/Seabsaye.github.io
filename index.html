The Aquavest

Developed to combat the consequenses associated with current methods of water collection within Sub-Saharan Africa, the Aquavest is a "vest-like" water-carrying apparatus. Now a finished product as of the completion of the high-fidelity design, the Aquavest has succeeded in meeting and surpassing goals such as minimizing the physical strain experienced by the user, decreasing levels of contaminents in the water collected, and increasing user mobility, effectively rendering a majority of the negatives corresponding with water collection in this region as non-existent.
